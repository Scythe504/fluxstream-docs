Fluxstream is a premium, self-hosted media client for torrent-based video archives. It is built as a **bring-your-own-media** player, functioning as a real-time playback engine. Paste a magnet link or connect to your own legal media feeds, and Fluxstream will parse the streaming swarm in real-time, allowing instant playback in a beautiful browser client.

<Callout type="info">
  Fluxstream does not index, host, or distribute copyrighted media files. It is an open-source tool designed for playing Creative Commons content, public archives, and personal torrent backups.
</Callout>

### Key Architectures

* **Real-time Streaming Engine**: Avoid waiting for full downloads. The client starts playback by prioritizing the sequential blocks needed for the video buffer.
* **Addon-friendly Architecture**: Register media providers or personal scraper feeds in seconds to group media collections cleanly.
* **Privacy First**: Fully self-hosted with no remote analytics, external trackers, or telemetry.
